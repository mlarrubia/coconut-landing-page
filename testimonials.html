Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup blue curaçao
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 8 cups ice
  • coconut flakes for garnish




Instructions

  1. Add small amount of cream of coconut and coconut flakes to two shallow dishes. Dip rim of glass into cream of coconut then into coconut flakes to rim glass.
  2. Add pineapple juice, blue curaçao, vodka, cream of coconut, and ice to blender. Blend on high until ice is crushed and desired consistency is reached.
  3. Pour cocktail into rimmed glass and serve immediately.
